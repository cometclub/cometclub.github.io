Why ComEt?

Any task becomes easier when it is divided amongst peers and collectively performed. We the members of the electronics community understand the importance of synergy and also the need to develop our skillset as the students of IIIT-NR now and as world-class professionals later on. Thus the community aims to bring together all the enthusiasts to share and explore the vivid diversity in the field of Electronics and Communication Engineering.

Targets and Scope :

Through the community, we aim to develop a culture in the campus that supports and promotes the growth of students into talented and skillful engineers with the ability to create and innovate new things. Also, we would like to develop a positive mentality towards the field of electronics and communication among our peers which is important as IIIT-NR is an institute that promotes growth in information technology. This would also further enhance the reputation and fame that our institute already has gained over years.

Prospective

The Community would provide the means for every student of IIIT-NR to learn explore and contribute to their knowledge as well as to several research ideas. All of the events conducted by the community will be taken care of and also the community will develop relations within and beyond the campus with eminent professors, intellectuals, corporations as well as research enthusiasts so that they could support the development of our students into world-class professionals.
